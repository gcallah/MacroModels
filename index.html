Macroeconomic Models

Some macroeconomic models in Excel. After you click on a link below, click on "View Raw" if you want to download the spreadsheet.

  • Real versus nominal growth

    Allows for easy generation of problems for differentiating real growth, nominal growth, and inflation in a two-good economy.
  • Keynesian Cross

    Experiment with the Keynesian Cross by setting various parameters for autonomous consumption, intended investment, and marginal propensity to consume.
  • Production Possibilities Frontier

    Demonstrate how the production possibility froniter can change and how various points along it are chosen.
